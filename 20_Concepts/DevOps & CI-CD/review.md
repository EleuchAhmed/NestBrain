# Review

A systematic evaluation of a desktop application's functionality, usability, and performance to identify defects, inconsistencies, or areas for improvement. Reviews may occur at various stages of development (e.g., code review, design review, UX review) and involve stakeholders such as developers, designers, or testers.

### Key Details:
- **Purpose**: Ensure quality, adherence to requirements, and alignment with best practices.
- **Types**:
  - **Code Review**: Peer examination of source code for errors, readability, and maintainability.
  - **Design Review**: Assessment of UI/UX, architecture, or system design.
  - **Usability Review**: Evaluation of user interaction and experience.
- **Methods**: Manual inspection, automated tools, or collaborative walkthroughs.
- **Outcomes**: Bug reports, feedback documentation, or iterative refinements.
