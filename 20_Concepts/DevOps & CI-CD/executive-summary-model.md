# Executive Summary Model

An **Executive Summary Model** is a concise, high-level overview of a desktop application's purpose, features, and value proposition. It serves as a quick reference for stakeholders, investors, or team members to understand the core aspects of the application without delving into technical details.

## Key Details
- **Purpose**: To provide a snapshot of the desktop application's goals, target audience, and unique selling points.
- **Components**: Typically includes the application's name, a brief description, key features, target market, and expected outcomes.
- **Use Case**: Often used in business plans, pitch decks, or project documentation to communicate essential information efficiently.
