# Review

A systematic evaluation or assessment of an AI agent's performance, design, or behavior to ensure alignment with intended goals, safety standards, and ethical guidelines. Reviews may involve testing, auditing, or peer analysis to identify strengths, weaknesses, or areas for improvement.

### Key Details:
- **Purpose**: Ensures reliability, safety, and efficacy of AI agents.
- **Methods**: Includes performance metrics, bias detection, and compliance checks.
- **Stakeholders**: Developers, ethicists, regulators, and end-users may participate.
- **Outcomes**: May lead to iterations, recalibrations, or deployment approvals.
