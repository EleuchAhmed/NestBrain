# Open Questions

### Definition
Open questions in the context of AI agents refer to unresolved or ongoing inquiries, challenges, or research areas that remain unanswered or require further exploration. These questions often drive innovation, guide research agendas, and highlight gaps in current understanding or capabilities of AI systems.

### Key Details
- **Research Gaps**: Areas where existing AI models, frameworks, or theories lack sufficient evidence, validation, or development.
- **Ethical Concerns**: Unresolved dilemmas such as bias, accountability, transparency, and the societal impact of AI agents.
- **Technical Challenges**: Limitations in scalability, robustness, adaptability, or interpretability of AI systems.
- **Interdisciplinary Needs**: Questions requiring collaboration across fields (e.g., neuroscience, philosophy, computer science) to advance AI agent development.
- **Future Directions**: Emerging topics like general AI, human-AI collaboration, or autonomous decision-making that lack definitive solutions.
